Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

CollectorOne for Collectors ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

All information collected by CollectorOne is provided by you and stored locally on your device by default. We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on our servers.

  • Photos and Videos: You may choose to add photos of your collectibles. These images are stored locally on your device.
  • Financial Information: You may enter purchase prices, estimated values, and other financial details about your items. This data is stored locally.
  • Other User Content: Item names, descriptions, notes, tags, custom fields, and collection names are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, including:

  • Displaying your collections and items
  • Generating statistics and charts
  • Exporting your data to CSV or JSON files

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate backend servers or cloud storage for your data.

When you export your data to CSV or JSON, the exported files are saved locally and shared through the standard iOS share sheet. You control where those files go.

4. Third-Party Services

CollectorOne does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

CollectorOne is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require an account or any personally identifiable information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
