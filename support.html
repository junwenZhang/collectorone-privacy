Common Topics

  • Data Export: You can export your collections to CSV or JSON from the Settings screen or any collection detail page.
  • Data Storage: All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is removed unless you have exported a backup.
  • Purchase Issues: CollectorOne is a one-time paid download. There are no subscriptions or in-app purchases. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support.
